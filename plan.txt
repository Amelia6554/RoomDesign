Phase 1 - Preparation
1. Find inspiration on social media.
2. Measure the room, noting all 3 dimensions.
3. Identify furniture to replace.
4. Search for and buy new furniture and flooring.
5. Plan the spatial layout for each new item.
6. Pick a paint color suitable for the new furniture.
7. Pack and remove all personal belongings.
8. Dispose of old furniture and move the rest to another room.

Phase 2 - Core Work
1. Fill all holes in the walls; protect windows and switches with masking tape.
2. Paint the room: 2 walls grey-blue, 2 walls light grey.
3. Remove old floor panels and install the new ones.

Phase 3 - Setup
1. Assemble the new furniture.
2. Place the furniture according to the spatial plan.

Phase 4 - Final Touches
1. Remove all protective tape from windows and switches.
2. Clean the entire space thoroughly.
3. Unpack and arrange all personal belongings on the shelves.
